The Platform

A different kind of dating app.
One that wants you to leave it.

01
AI Onboarding Interview
No questionnaire. An AI conversation builds a richer psychological profile than any form ever could. People open up when they're talking, not filling out boxes.
02
Subconscious Type Learning
Swipe photos naturally. No labels, no categories. Your real type emerges from behavior — not what you say you want, but what you actually respond to.
03
AI-to-AI Pre-Vetting
Your AI and their AI negotiate compatibility before either human interacts. Sensitive preferences — handled. No awkward disclosures. No guesswork.
04
Your First Second Date
An in-app video call is required before the venue unlocks. This IS the first date. When you meet in person, you're already on your second. Zero strangers. Zero catfish.
05
Date Night Concierge
Set a "Home By" time. The app delivers the message gracefully so you never have to. Running late? It notifies them. Want to leave early? One tap. The social buffer for everything nobody wants to say out loud.
06
Safety Widget
Geofenced to your booked venue. Silent check-ins every hour. No response in 10 minutes = treated as a red. Your trusted contact gets location, profile, photo, and date details. Silence is the trigger.
07
Phone Nudge System
Passive in-date monitoring with escalating sass. Heavy users get quietly matched together — if you're both showing each other memes in 10 minutes, that's your love language. The algorithm figured it out.
08
Post Date Review
AI debrief after every date. A gap map is built — what was present, what was missing. Every next match is a targeted correction. Anonymized and fed back into the network. Every date everywhere improves everyone's next one.
09
Encrypted Local Memory
All psychological data lives on-device, encrypted. Nothing in the cloud. Panic button wipes everything instantly. Your profile belongs to you — not us.
