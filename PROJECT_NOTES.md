# Second Date — Project Notes
*Conceived April 8-9, 2026 in a single brainstorm session*

## The Name
**Second Date** — every in-person meeting is technically a second date. The video call IS the first date. You never meet a stranger. You meet someone you already know.

## Brand Voice
- App name: **Second Date**
- Campaign: **"Stop Guessing. Start Connecting."**
- Tagline: **"Every first meeting feels like a second date."**
- Alt tagline: **"Skip the awkward part."**
- Brand personality voice: **"I Got You"** — warm, slightly sassy matchmaker friend
- Company name candidate: **Just Date-a**

## The Core Insight
YouTube Music doesn't ask what you like. It figures it out. 70% of YouTube watch time comes from algorithm recommendations — not searches. We built that for people. The algorithm learns your real type (not your stated type) from behavior, not declaration.

## The Competitive Position
- Every other dating app is optimizing for engagement — keeping you on app = keeping you single
- Second Date succeeds ONLY when you do
- The average dater uses 3+ apps simultaneously — proof that none of them work
- "Finally. A dating app that matches you with someone who also can't put their phone down."

## Core Features

### 1. AI Onboarding Interview
No profile. No form. AI conversation builds psychological profile richer than any questionnaire.

### 2. Subconscious Type Learning
Swipe photos naturally. No categories. No labels. After enough swipes, unbiased pattern emerges — your real type, not your performed type.

### 3. AI-to-AI Pre-Vetting
Both users' AIs negotiate compatibility before humans interact. Sensitive preferences handled without either person disclosing directly.

### 4. Encrypted Local Memory
All psychological data on-device, encrypted. Nothing in cloud. Panic button wipes everything instantly.

### 5. Your First Second Date (Video Gate)
In-app video call required before venue unlocks. This IS the first date. When you meet in person it's already the second. After call: "Congratulations. You just had your first date. Now let's plan the second one." Zero catfish. Guaranteed.

### 6. Date Night Concierge
Book date in-app. Set "Home By" time — app delivers the message gracefully so you never have to. Running late? App notifies them. Want to leave early? One tap. The social buffer for everything nobody wants to say out loud.

### 7. Safety Date Widget
- Geofence tied to booked venue — app already knows where you're supposed to be
- Both phones get silent red/green check-in button every hour
- No response in 10 minutes = treated as red
- Trusted contact gets: location, profile, photo, date details
- If location diverges unexpectedly from venue — ping fires automatically
- Silence is the trigger

### 8. Phone Nudge System
In-date passive phone monitoring. Escalating sass:
1. "Don't forget there's a real human sitting across from you."
2. "Seriously. Put it down. We didn't do all this work for you to check likes."
3. "Your match just reported you're on your phone. Just kidding. But also... put it down."

**The Doom Scroller Match:** Heavy phone users get quietly matched together. First date — both phones out in 10 minutes, showing each other memes. Neither one cares. That's their love language. The algorithm figured it out without either of them saying a word.

### 9. Post Date Review (PDR)
AI debrief after every date. Gap map built — what was present, what was missing. Next match is a targeted correction, not a random pull. Anonymized and fed back into network. Every date anywhere improves everyone's next recommendation.

### 10. Date Night Intelligence
PDR data reveals where successful connections happen. Venue suggestions backed by real outcome data from your city. "Second Date Certified" venue licensing — venues pay for the badge because it drives reservations.

## Revenue Streams
1. **Subscription** — $29-49/mo, premium tier
2. **Brand Intelligence** — anonymized PDR data to fragrance, fashion, hospitality brands. First-party emotional outcome data. No comparable dataset exists.
3. **Venue Certification** — "Second Date Certified" annual licensing fee. Zero incremental cost.

## The Third Date Promise
- PDR asks both parties privately: "Would you go out again?"
- Both say yes → entered into weekly Third Date lottery
- Second Date covers the full cost of date three at a curated venue
- Lottery mechanic keeps engagement high — not everyone wins but everyone wonders
- Winners post, tag, tell everyone — product IS the promotion
- Date arc: First Date (video call) → Second Date (in-person) → Third Date (maybe on us)

## IP / Launch Strategy Notes
